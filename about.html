Who is Tail Africa Tours?

Tail Africa Tours is an inbound tour operator in Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe. We specialize in small groupnguided tours, and custom made tours in Southern Africa.We ensure that our guests experience the best of what Africa has to offer. Our goal is to make sure your expedition is as seamless as possible, and we fully leverage the collective experience, passion and commitment of our team to see to all your travel needs. When it comes to the fascinating continent of Africa, we confidently offer a wealth of expertise and knowledge gained from over 12 years of experience on the ground. This is what differentiates us from the rest. Talk to us about making your dream of a thrilling African adventure a reality.
